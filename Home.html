Good Shepherd Sisters

The Good Shepherd Sisters of Sri Lanka has started their services for over 175 years. Although their main service seems to be helping Parish work, they are doing lot more services to the people who are needy. Some of the services they are providing are education for children, providing shelter for children seeking probationary assistance for various reasons, providing shelter for

unmarried mothers and taking care of their unborn and introducing a good parent for the babies after born, intervene and take care of women who are helpless due to various reasons such as alcoholism, due to raping and prostitution. Among all these services Community Based Child Protection is one of these big projects.

CBCP

The future of a country will depend on the children living today. If the children are directed correctly, educated, and cared well today, the country’s future will flourish. So, our responsibility today is to educate and protect the children well, but that is not what is happening today. If the majority of children give up education and do jobs with their parents, their lives will not be secure.

Community Base child protection program’s aim is to protect children. Here they concentrate on children in coastal areas and in tea estates. Instead of keeping the children in Children’s Homes, the project will try to achieve the objectives by visiting communities where they are, that is the villages and developing them with the help of the village community. To develop the children, first their families must be uplifted economically. Therefore, under this project, they provide skill training for the parents of the children to empower the parents, and seek the assistance of the Police in the area, Grama Sevaka, and the AG office in order to provide security for the children.
